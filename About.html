MEET US

There was a time where we as youth felt that we have been
too busy in our busy schedule that we barely had any time
left for our physical and mental well being.
We knew that we had to sort it out so as to live a healthy lifestyle as well as
encourage other people like us to be fit.
But it was a bit challenging for us as people were not well aware
about what it meant to be fit.
There were a lot of misconceptions regarding fitness.
People thought that being skinny and thin meant to be fit.

People didn't know what they were doing in the name of fitness.
They had no direction and no plan, they were making a lot of mistakes.
So we started to learn about nutrition and making slightly
better food choices ourselves and encouraged others as well.
We learnt about training plans and made sure that
the time we were spending in the gym was effective.

Then, we decided to spread awareness in this field thereby creating "Fitfreaks".
Fitfreaks is the vision of Disha Singh to unravel some of the least
understood aspects of fitness and lifestyle with
a wonderful and enthusiastic team consisting of
the very dedicated Naman Bansal who designed the layout with his incredible knowledge,
Devesh Kartiken Sharma who helped us with his fascinating ideas.
Muskan Thakur who added her amazing skills to the project.
You all did an amazing job of instilling the power of hard work in you.

"Mind over matter – focus your mind, your body will follow
and you’ll be amazed at what you actually achieve."

~ A very smart person

Why To Choose FitFreaks

At FitFreaks, We want to help men and women that have felt like
they've been spinning their wheels with their fitness and health.
We grasp that different diets can seem attractive and trying various solutions to find the style of eating
that's right for you seems essential - but it can also slow
your progress down while you are spending time on trial and error.

We launched FitFreaks, Personal Training in Chandigarh, so We can help people cut to the chase
and find what works for them best as a result of using science-backed methods and
individualisation protocols that will bring the results they desire.
If you've just started working out, but you'd like to make the best out of your sessions,
we are here to help. If you've been training for some time but feel like you're stuck with your progress,
we will show you how to overcome the plateau and start seeing results again.
